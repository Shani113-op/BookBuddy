Our Story

Book Recommendation was founded in 2024 by Shani Sharma, a book enthusiast who wanted to make it easier for readers to discover great books. Starting as a passion project, it has now grown into a thriving platform with thousands of users worldwide.

Our Values

  • Passion for Reading: We believe in the power of books to inspire, educate, and entertain.
  • Innovation: We're constantly exploring new technologies and algorithms to improve our recommendation system.
  • User-Centric Approach: We prioritize the needs and preferences of our users in everything we do.
  • Diversity and Inclusivity: We strive to offer a diverse range of books and ensure that our platform is inclusive to all readers.
